Ellison Homestead

We're on a mission to live a more simple and sustainable life whilst serving some of the world's deepest needs.

We met volunteering on a kibbutz in Israel in 2011. Since then we've followed our passion to help make the world a better place. From starting grassroots recycling initiatives in Egypt to designing and building classrooms for children with special needs in India, we've been blessed with a life worth living and want to help provide others with the same.
