Ellison Homestead

We're on a mission to live a more simple and sustainable life whilst serving some of the world's deepest needs.

We met volunteering on a kibbutz in Israel in 2011. Since then we've adventured together across the world. From starting grassroots recycling initiatives in Egypt to designing and building classrooms for children with special needs in India, we've followed our passion to help make the world a better place.
